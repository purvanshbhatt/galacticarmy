FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the galactic army club ?

The Galactic Army Club is an exclusive NFT project featuring 3333 unique pieces of art. We are aiming to create an unmatched community where holders gain exclusive access to physical and digital events around the world and in the Metaverse and the community wallet.

How many Warriors will be available?

A total of 3333 warriors will be available for the public. The other battalions will be joining the army soon

What blockchain will the Warriors be minted on?

The Warriors are stored on the Ethereum blockchain and are compliant of the ERC-721 contract of the Ethereum blockchain.

How rare is each Warrior, and how are traits determined?

Each Warrior is 100% unique and is generated from 90+ hand drawn traits.

Where can I view my NFT?

Once minted, you'll just need to login into your OpenSea account to view your NFTs.

How can I mint my warrior?

You will need a Metamask wallet or a Wallet Connect compatible wallet.

What makes us different?

We are aiming to build a community where all our holders earn high utility by being a part of this project. As a club we are building the strongest community of the Metaverse and all our holders will get numerous benefits and giveaways as a token of appreciation. All our initial supporters and whitelists will be getting their minting fees refunded.
